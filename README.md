# 🚛 Logistics Operations Optimization Analysis (2022–2024)

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents a comprehensive logistics operations analysis focused on improving:

- Delivery performance
- Fleet utilization
- Driver efficiency
- Route profitability
- Maintenance operations

The analysis was performed entirely using **SQL** for:
- data cleaning,
- transformation,
- feature engineering,
- and business analysis.

**Power BI** was used exclusively for dashboard visualization and storytelling.

---

# 🎯 Business Problem

The logistics company experienced operational challenges including:

- High delivery delays
- Rising maintenance costs
- Uneven fleet utilization
- Route inefficiencies
- Variations in driver performance

The objective of this project was to identify operational inefficiencies and provide actionable recommendations that improve:
- profitability,
- operational efficiency,
- and customer satisfaction.

# 🔍 Key Insights

## 1. Delivery Performance is a Major Concern
- Only **44.60%** of deliveries were completed on time.
- Over **61%** of shipments experienced delays.
- Deliveries to **California**, **Washington**, **Texas**, and **Oregon** accounted for over **30%** of the late deliveries.

### Most delay-prone routes:
- Ohio → California
- Texas → Indiana
- Oregon → Washington

---

## 2. Some Routes Are Profitable But Operationally Risky
Certain routes generated high profits while also recording:
- high delays,
- and high operational costs.

---

## 3. Volvo Trucks Were the Best Performing Fleet Assets
Volvo trucks:
- generated the highest profit,
- completed the most trips,
- but also incurred the highest maintenance cost.

---

## 4. Driver Performance Varied Significantly
- Experienced drivers have a better on-time delivery rate.
- Top profit-generating drivers were not always the most punctual.

This revealed the need for:
- a weighted driver performance scoring model.

---

## 5. Maintenance Costs Were Substantial
- Total maintenance cost exceeded **$3.87bn**
- Average downtime reached **13.62 hours**
- Maintenance cost was lowest in February, the low number of trips completed in this month explains this.

---

# 💡 Recommendations

## ✅ Improve Route Scheduling
Optimize high-delay routes using:
- better dispatch timing,
- and route optimization strategies.

---

## ✅ Implement Predictive Maintenance
Use:
- mileage,
- downtime,
- and maintenance history
to predict breakdowns before failure occurs.

---

## ✅ Develop Driver Performance Scores
Create weighted driver metrics using:
- punctuality,
- profitability,
- safety,
- and fuel efficiency.

---

## ✅ Balance Fleet Utilization
Reduce over-dependence on specific trucks to:
- lower maintenance pressure,
- and extend asset lifespan.

---

## ✅ Monitor Route Profitability Continuously
Track:
- profit,
- delays,
- maintenance,
- and fuel cost together.
